Canada ... Our home and native land!
Welcome to our website to celebrate being Canadian.
From our universal health care to our beautiful scenery to our multicultural society, we are fortunate to live in one of the best countries in the world.
Canada has been inhabited by aboriginal people for thousands of years. In the late 15th century, British and French expeditions brought explores and settlers along the Atlantic coast who then spread westward. Today Canada has a diverse makeup of people from all over the world.
